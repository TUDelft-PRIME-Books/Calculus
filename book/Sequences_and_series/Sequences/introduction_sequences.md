In {numref}`Sec:SumsAndProducts` we have seen _finite_ sequences of real numbers. In this chapter we generalise these to _infinite_ sequences. Here, the concept of _convergence_ appears to be very important.

We start of with the definition and properties of various kinds of infinite sequences. Then we will focus on the convergence and we end with iterative methods for various problems, which are based on infinite sequences.
